Inspire the World with Broader Impacts

The idea of reaching the public, especially kids, is inspiring. But it also sounds like a lot of work. You’re busy with your research and the demands of academic life. With broader impacts making up a significant part of most grants, you need a plan that is manageable.

Complexity doesn't scare us.

We embrace the beauty of complexity

One of the cool things about our team is its ability to dig into a crazy diverse array of topics--including folklore, ecology, thermodynamics, music, art, business, history, neuroscience, mathematics, biology, creative writing, medicine, and genetics.

We are people who love learning. David grew up reading the encyclopedia for fun. Our lead game designer Phil, when asked how he knows so much, replied, “Isn’t it my job to know about everything?”

Partner with a team that gets it

Why Academics Partner with Field Day

Most researchers hire a single person or small team of novices that can’t deliver. Others hire expensive design firms that don’t understand the research process, cost a ton of money and deliver with mixed results.

At Field Day, we work with you to bring your awesome research to life. We use everything from virtual reality and apps to video games, augmented reality and art. Our team will join you as partners in grant-writing and co-design. We specialize in working with academics to connect the public with what inspires you.
